## Who Is Coming to This Page?

### Segment 1: The New Tenant / New Homeowner (35%)
- **Who they are:** Just moved into a new apartment or bought a house in Brooklyn
- **Age:** 22–45, renters or first-time buyers
- **Situation:** Moving into a new place, wants to rekey locks before old tenant's keys float around the neighborhood
- **Urgency:** Moderate — not locked out, but wants to schedule soon
- **Device:** Mixed desktop/mobile
- **Pain:** "Who had keys to this apartment before me?" + "This is a new city — who do I trust?"
- **Decision driver:** Local business, transparent pricing, availability, friendly tone
- **What wins them:** Rekeying explanation (why it's smart, affordable), how to schedule

### Segment 2: The Security Upgrader (25%)
- **Who they are:** Brooklyn homeowner or long-term renter wanting better locks
- **Age:** 30–60
- **Situation:** Recent break-in in the neighborhood, wants deadbolt upgrade or smart lock
- **Urgency:** Low-to-moderate — researching options
- **Device:** Desktop preferred
- **Pain:** "What's actually secure? I don't want to overspend but I also don't want a flimsy lock."
- **Decision driver:** Product knowledge, expert recommendation, price transparency
- **What wins them:** Specific product examples, smart lock vs. deadbolt comparison, pricing ranges

### Segment 3: The Locked-Out Resident (25%)
- **Who they are:** Locked out of their apartment or house — not a full emergency but needs help today
- **Age:** Any
- **Device:** Mobile
- **Situation:** Left keys inside, key broke in lock, lock jammed
- **Decision driver:** How fast, how much, are they legitimate
- **What wins them:** Same-day availability, phone number, transparent pricing

### Segment 4: The Landlord / Property Manager (15%)
- **Who they are:** Brooklyn landlord with 1–20 units
- **Age:** 35–65
- **Situation:** Tenant moved out, needs rekey; tenant locked out; wants master key system
- **Pain:** Wants a reliable locksmith they can call repeatedly, not a one-time scam
- **Decision driver:** Reliability, commercial-grade service at reasonable price, can handle multiple units
- **What wins them:** Master key system explanation, recurring service mention, professional tone
