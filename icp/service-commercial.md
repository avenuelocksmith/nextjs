## Who Is Coming to This Page?

### Segment 1: The Small Business Owner (40%)
- **Who they are:** Owner of a retail shop, restaurant, salon, or small office in Brooklyn or NYC
- **Age:** 30–60
- **Situation:** Need to upgrade security, had an employee leave and want to rekey, or experienced a break-in
- **Urgency:** Moderate — not always an emergency, but wants it resolved quickly
- **Device:** Mix of mobile and desktop
- **Pain:** "I can't have my business vulnerable. I also can't afford to shut down for a day."
- **Decision driver:** Fast, minimal downtime, knows commercial locks, can invoice/receipt
- **What wins them:** Commercial-specific experience, industries served list, after-hours emergency availability

### Segment 2: The Property Manager (30%)
- **Who they are:** Manages 1–50 commercial or mixed-use properties in Brooklyn
- **Age:** 35–55
- **Situation:** Tenant changeover, break-in, master key system needed, access control upgrade
- **Pain:** "I need a locksmith I can call for multiple properties and trust they'll do consistent work"
- **Device:** Desktop mostly
- **Decision driver:** Reliability, invoicing capability, can handle scale, master key systems
- **What wins them:** Master key system explanation, multi-property experience, repeat client service

### Segment 3: The Office Manager / Operations Person (20%)
- **Who they are:** Manages an office in Brooklyn or Manhattan, tasked with security by their employer
- **Age:** 25–45
- **Situation:** Keycard system issue, employee lost a key fob, wants to upgrade to access control
- **Pain:** "I need to look professional presenting this solution to my boss"
- **Device:** Desktop
- **Decision driver:** Brand credibility, professional appearance, clear service descriptions, ability to provide documentation
- **What wins them:** Industries served, access control systems explained clearly, professional tone

### Segment 4: The Business Owner After a Break-In (10%)
- **Who they are:** Business was broken into last night or recently
- **Urgency:** HIGH — emergency tier
- **Device:** Mobile
- **Pain:** "We need to board up, change locks, and upgrade security TODAY"
- **Decision driver:** Can you come now? Do you do emergency commercial service?
- **What wins them:** Emergency availability, "burglary repair" mentioned prominently, fast response
