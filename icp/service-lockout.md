## Who Is Coming to These Pages?

These pages are landing points for people who searched specifically for "lockout service" (rather than "emergency locksmith" or a specific service type). The intent is identical to emergency pages but the keyword funnel is slightly different.

### Primary Visitor: Locked Out Person (75%)
- **Situation:** Locked out of home, business, or car — needs help immediately
- **Device:** 90%+ mobile
- **Emotional state:** Frustrated, stressed, possibly panicking
- **What they need:** Phone number, response time, "we can help now"
- **What they do NOT need:** Long educational content about lockout services

### Secondary Visitor: Property Manager Setting Up Protocols (15%)
- **Situation:** Wants a locksmith on speed dial for tenant lockouts
- **Device:** Desktop
- **What they need:** Reliability, recurring service capability, professional tone

### Tertiary Visitor: Researcher / Planner (10%)
- **Situation:** Had a lockout before, wants to be prepared
- **Device:** Mixed
- **What they need:** Process explanation, pricing range, reviews
