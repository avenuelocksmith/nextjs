## Who Reads the Blog?

### Primary: The Informational Searcher (60%)
- **Who they are:** Someone researching a locksmith-related topic, not yet ready to buy
- **Query examples:** "how to choose a locksmith nyc", "what is lock rekeying", "how to secure apartment NYC"
- **Device:** Mix
- **Behavior:** Reading the article, potentially following internal links to service pages
- **Pain:** They have a question, want an honest answer
- **Conversion path:** Blog post → service page → contact/call

### Secondary: The Trust-Builder (30%)
- **Who they are:** Someone who found Avenue Locksmith on Google or referral and is reading blog content to evaluate expertise
- **Behavior:** Checking if the company "knows their stuff"
- **What converts them:** Authoritative, accurate, helpful content that demonstrates real expertise

### Tertiary: The Return Visitor (10%)
- **Who they are:** Past customer, curious about related topics
- **Behavior:** Engaged reader, brand-loyal
- **Conversion:** Referral generation, review prompt

## Blog Content Strategy

### Google E-E-A-T for Local Service Blog Content

Blog posts must demonstrate:
- **Experience:** "In our experience servicing Brooklyn apartments..." / "We've seen this issue hundreds of times..."
- **Expertise:** Specific technical knowledge (lock grades, pick resistance, transponder programming)
- **Authoritativeness:** Links to trusted sources (ALOA, NYC housing laws, lock manufacturers)
- **Trustworthiness:** Author attribution, date published, date updated, no sponsored/misleading claims

### Post Structure (Template F)

```
Breadcrumb navigation
Article H1 — specific, search-optimized
Meta info: Author name | Published date | Updated date | Category | Reading time

Featured image (alt text required)

Table of Contents (for 1,000+ word posts)

Body Content:
  H2: [Introduction — answer the question in the first 100 words]
  H2: [Subtopic 1]
  H2: [Subtopic 2]
  H2: [Subtopic 3]
  H2: [How Avenue Locksmith Can Help] — soft CTA section
  H2: [Conclusion / Summary]

In-content CTA: "Need a locksmith in Brooklyn? Call (347) 386-7164"

Author Bio: "Written by the Avenue Locksmith team — licensed locksmiths serving Brooklyn since [year]."

Related Posts (3 links)

Bottom CTA: "Get Expert Help Now" + Phone + Form
```

## Content Writer Notes
- Blog posts are NOT product pitches — they must be genuinely helpful first
- Always cite sources for legal claims (NYC tenant law, building codes)
- Never post content that could constitute legal advice — use "consult a lawyer" where appropriate
- Target 1,000–1,500 words for standard posts, 1,500–2,000 for comprehensive guides
- Include at least one practical takeaway the reader can use even if they never call Avenue Locksmith — this is what builds trust
- Update posts regularly (at least annually) — Google values freshness on local service topics
- Author attribution matters for E-E-A-T — "Written by the Avenue Locksmith team" is minimum; named author is better
