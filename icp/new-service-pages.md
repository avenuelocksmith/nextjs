## /services/lock-rekey/

### Who Is Coming
- **Primary (55%):** Homeowner or renter who just moved into a new apartment — wants to invalidate old keys
- **Secondary (30%):** Landlord after tenant move-out
- **Tertiary (15%):** Someone who lost a key or had a key stolen

### Pain Points
1. "I don't know if rekeying is safe enough or if I need to replace the whole lock"
2. "I don't know how much it costs — I don't want to be surprised"
3. "I need this done quickly, ideally today"

### Key Differentiators for Content
- Rekeying = changing the internal pins so old keys no longer work — same lock, new keys
- Much cheaper than replacing the entire lock ($50–$100 vs $125–$300)
- Takes 15–30 minutes per lock
- Smart move: moving into a new apartment, after a breakup/roommate change, after losing a key

### SEO Target: "lock rekey brooklyn", "rekey apartment brooklyn", "rekey locks brooklyn ny"

## /new-tenant-lock-change/

### Who Is Coming
**This is one of the highest-opportunity missing pages — Brooklyn has one of the highest renter populations in the US.**
- **Primary (70%):** Someone who just moved into a new Brooklyn apartment
- **Secondary (20%):** Person whose roommate moved out and wants to secure the apartment
- **Tertiary (10%):** Landlord setting policy for new tenants

### Why This Page Is Critical
"Lock change new apartment" and "should I change locks when I move in" are extremely high-volume informational + transactional searches. This page targets the moment of maximum intent — someone just signed a lease and is thinking about security for the first time.

### Pain Points
1. "I don't know who has keys to my new apartment — the previous tenant, building staff, their friends?"
2. "My landlord said they'll rekey it — should I trust that or do it myself?"
3. "Is this even legal — can I change the locks in my rented apartment?"
4. "How much does it cost to change locks in an apartment?"

### NYC Tenant Rights Note (Critical for E-E-A-T)
In New York, tenants have the right to install additional locks on their apartment doors (with some restrictions). Content should address this — it builds trust and establishes legal credibility. Note: always advise the reader to check their lease and consult their landlord.

### Content Structure
- H1: "New Tenant Lock Change in Brooklyn, NY — Secure Your New Apartment"
- Why you should change/rekey on move-in (addresses previous key holders)
- Rekeying vs. replacing — which makes more sense for a renter
- NYC tenant rights overview (with disclaimer to verify with landlord/lease)
- How the process works (we come to you, done in 30 minutes)
- Price: rekeying from $75, lock change from $125
- FAQ: Can I change locks as a renter in NYC? Will my landlord be upset? Do I need to give my landlord a copy of the new key?

### SEO Target: "lock change new apartment brooklyn", "move in lock change brooklyn", "should I change locks new apartment nyc", "rekey apartment when moving in brooklyn"
