## Who Is Coming to These Pages?

### Segment 1: The Proactive Security Upgrader — Homeowner (30%)
- **Who they are:** Brooklyn homeowner or long-term renter, recent crime nearby, or just moved in and wants better security
- **Age:** 30–60
- **Device:** Desktop or mobile (research phase)
- **Urgency:** Low-to-moderate — planning, not emergency
- **Pain:** "I don't know what's actually secure vs. marketing hype. I don't want to overspend."
- **Decision driver:** Expert guidance, real product recommendations, pricing transparency
- **What wins them:** Educational content, comparison of options, consultation offer

### Segment 2: The Business Owner Upgrading Security (35%)
- **Who they are:** Small business owner in Brooklyn wanting access control, CCTV, or high-security locks
- **Age:** 30–55
- **Device:** Desktop
- **Urgency:** Moderate — often triggered by a security incident, insurance requirement, or growth
- **Pain:** "What do I actually need? I don't want to over-engineer this or get locked into a system I can't maintain."
- **Decision driver:** Clear ROI, system compatibility, professional installation, support after install
- **What wins them:** Business case for security investment, industries served, system integration mention

### Segment 3: Tech-Forward Buyer — Smart Locks / Keyless (20%)
- **Who they are:** Tech-savvy person who wants smart home integration or keyless convenience
- **Age:** 25–45
- **Device:** Mobile or desktop
- **Pain:** "Which smart lock actually works reliably? Does this locksmith know about the specific brand I want?"
- **Decision driver:** Product knowledge, mentions of specific brands (August, Schlage, Yale, etc.)
- **What wins them:** Technology-forward language, brand mentions, integration capability (Alexa, Google Home, Apple HomeKit)

### Segment 4: Post-Break-In Security Repair (15%)
- **Who they are:** Home or business recently broken into — needs emergency reinforcement AND upgraded security
- **Urgency:** High
- **Device:** Mobile
- **Pain:** "I need my place secured NOW and I need to prevent this from happening again"
- **Decision driver:** Speed, can bundle emergency repair with upgrade, trust
- **What wins them:** Mention burglary repair + upgrade path in the same sentence

## Individual Sub-Page ICPs

### High-Security Locks
- **Visitor:** Homeowner or business owner who wants the best physical lock
- **Key message:** Grade 1 ANSI locks, pick-resistant, drill-resistant, bump-resistant
- **Brand examples:** Medeco, Mul-T-Lock, ASSA Abloy
- **Content focus:** What makes a lock "high security", when you need it, cost vs. benefit
- **H1:** "High-Security Lock Installation in Brooklyn, NY"

### Access Control Systems
- **Visitor:** Business owner or property manager wanting controlled entry
- **Key message:** Replace physical keys with scalable electronic systems — manage who gets in and when
- **Content focus:** Keycard, keypad, app-based; scalable from 1 door to 50 doors; audit trail capability
- **H1:** "Access Control Systems in Brooklyn, NY — Commercial & Residential"

### CCTV Installation
- **Visitor:** Homeowner or business owner wanting surveillance coverage
- **Key message:** Professional installation (not DIY), integrated with access control if desired, remote monitoring
- **Content focus:** Camera types, indoor vs. outdoor, storage/cloud options, monitoring
- **H1:** "CCTV Installation in Brooklyn, NY — Professional Security Camera Systems"

### Smart Locks
- **Visitor:** Tech-forward homeowner or short-term rental host (Airbnb/VRBO)
- **Key message:** Convenient, compatible with smart home systems, no more lost keys
- **Content focus:** How smart locks work, compatibility (iOS/Android, Alexa, Google Home), best use cases
- **H1:** "Smart Lock Installation in Brooklyn, NY — Keyless Convenience for Your Home"

### Keyless Entry Systems
- **Visitor:** Business or homeowner who wants no keys but not necessarily app-controlled
- **Key message:** Keypad entry — simple, reliable, no smartphone required
- **Content focus:** Keypad vs. smart lock difference, best for multi-person households/businesses
- **H1:** "Keyless Entry System Installation in Brooklyn, NY"

### Biometric Access Systems
- **Visitor:** High-security need — executive office, medical facility, high-value home
- **Key message:** Fingerprint/facial recognition — highest security, no keys or codes to lose
- **Content focus:** How biometric works, where it makes sense, cost (premium product), integration
- **H1:** "Biometric Access System Installation in Brooklyn, NY"

### Reinforced Door & Frame Systems
- **Visitor:** Homeowner in area with break-in history, or business owner with forced entry concern
- **Key message:** 70% of burglaries happen through the door — reinforcing the frame matters more than the lock
- **Content focus:** Door frame reinforcement kits, steel door frames, door barricade bars, multi-point locking
- **H1:** "Reinforced Door & Frame Systems in Brooklyn, NY — Stop Forced Entry"
