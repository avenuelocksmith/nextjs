## Who Is Coming to This Page?

### Primary Visitor: The Locked-Out Person (70% of traffic)
- **Who they are:** Someone who is locked out RIGHT NOW — from their home, car, or office
- **Age:** Any — 18 to 70+
- **Device:** 90%+ mobile, one hand, possibly outside in the cold/dark/rain
- **Emotional state:** Stressed, frustrated, possibly scared (especially if it's nighttime)
- **Time on page:** Under 20 seconds — they are not reading content, they are looking for a phone number
- **Biggest fear:** Calling a scam locksmith who shows up, does 5 minutes of work, and charges $500
- **What immediately wins them:** Large visible phone number, "30 minutes", "no hidden fees", "licensed"

### Secondary Visitor: The Planner (20% of traffic)
- **Who they are:** Someone who experienced a lockout recently or knows someone who did. They want to save a locksmith number "just in case"
- **Device:** Desktop or mobile
- **Behavior:** Reading the page, checking reviews, comparing options
- **What wins them:** Credentials, service breadth, reviews, how the process works

### Tertiary Visitor: The Business Owner (10% of traffic)
- **Who they are:** Property manager or small business owner setting up emergency protocols
- **What wins them:** After-hours availability, commercial locks handled, professional reliability
