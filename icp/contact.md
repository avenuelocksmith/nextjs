# ICP: Contact Page
**URL:** `https://www.avenuelocks.com/contact/`
**Template:** Custom — minimal, functional
**Page Goal:** Make it as easy as possible for someone to reach Avenue Locksmith via their preferred method.

---

## Who Is Coming to This Page?

### Primary: The Form Submitter (40%)
- **Who they are:** Non-emergency — wants a quote, has a question, or prefers not to call
- **Device:** Mix
- **What they need:** Simple, fast form — name, phone, service type, message
- **Pain:** Long or confusing forms cause drop-off

### Secondary: The Phone Searcher (35%)
- **Who they are:** Came to the contact page specifically to find the phone number
- **Device:** Mobile
- **What they need:** Phone number as the FIRST thing on the page — tap to call
- **Note:** These visitors may have lost the phone number from the header — don't make them search

### Tertiary: The Email / Address Verifier (25%)
- **Who they are:** Wants to verify the business is real, confirm address, or send an email
- **What they need:** NAP displayed clearly (Name, Address, Phone, Email)
